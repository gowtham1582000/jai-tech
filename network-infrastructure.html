Switches

You can build or expand your Ethernet network between your computers by using a network switch cabinet, which is made to hold and preserve your network switch equipment. These can be used to store switches, patch panels, routers, and other pieces of networking hardware.

Given that they must carry fewer equipment than server rack cabinets, network switch cabinets are smaller in size. Our selection of network switch cabinets is a closed system that helps shield the equipment within from debris and other outside elements. As the equipment stored inside them is not susceptible to overheating like server equipment and fan units are not necessary, network switch cabinets often do not have perforated or vented panels.
