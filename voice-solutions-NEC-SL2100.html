NEC SL2100

Today’s mobile workers depend on communication tools that accommodate flexible workspaces and allow free roaming wherever they are. The SL2100 ensures your team stays connected but without escalating business mobile costs. The SL2100 is highly cost effective because there is more built-in.

  • Audio Conferencing

  • Auto Attendant

  • Mobility / BYOD / Remote Extension
  • Music on Hold

  • Unified Messaging

With VoIP capabilities and a complete Unified Communications (UC) client available right from your web browser, it puts UC within reach of small business. It allows your business to function no matter where your staff are with high quality desk phones for any scenario, the ability to pair your desk phone with your mobile, and softphones for both your PC and your smartphone. The SL2100 is built with small business in mind and can be utilised by the whole organization; everyone from receptionists to customer support staff. The SL2100 is ideal for Small/Medium organizations, Small Hotels/ Motels and Healthcare applications.

  • Video Conferencing & Collaboration
  • Voice Mail
  • VoIP Enabled
  • Web-Based Unified Communication Client
